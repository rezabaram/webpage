Deposition of ellipsoidal particles
Reza M. Baram, Pedro G. Lind

In this work we do a systematic study on the role of the shape and the size of constituting particles in determining properties of packings of ellipsoidal particles constructed through sequential depositions. For this purpose, we develop an efficient method for calculation of the contact force which is used in molecular dynamics simulations these systems. We also discuss our results from the point of view of their possible applications in modeling porous media and sedimentation processes.
