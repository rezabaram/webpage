Space-filling bearings in three dimensions
R. Mahmoodi Baram, Hans J. Herrmann and N. Rivier

In this work, we present the first space-filling bearing in three dimensions. It is shown that a packing which contains only loops with even number of spheres can be constructed in a self-similar way and that it can act as a three dimensional bearing in which spheres can rotate without slip and with negligible torsion friction.
